About Us

WELCOME TO SWEETTREATS BAKERY ONLINE

At SweetTreats Bakery, we believe every bite should be a moment of joy. Since 1999, our ovens have been whipping up more than just desserts—we bake memories. From the first whiff of buttery croissants fresh at dawn to the last crumb of a midnight cupcake craving, we’re here to turn your everyday moments into something extraordinary.

Our Story: What began as a tiny corner shop with a single oven has blossomed into a community hub for sugar-dusted dreams. Founded by master baker Silindile Mnisi, SweetTreats started with a simple mission: craft irresistible treats that taste like home. Today, our team of passionate bakers still follows her original recipes—just with a sprinkle of modern magic.

What Makes Us Special:
✨ Handcrafted with Love: Every flaky pastry, gooey cinnamon roll, and crusty sourdough loaf is shaped by hand, never mass-produced.
✨ Farm-to-Oven Ingredients: We source locally whenever possible—think free-range eggs, European butter, and seasonal fruits.
✨ For Every Occasion: Birthdays, weddings, or "just because" days—our treats are designed to delight. (Psst… ask about our custom cakes!)

The Future is Sweet: As we prepare to open our first international locations, we’re thrilled to share our passion globally. But no matter how far we go, SweetTreats will always stay true to its roots: quality over quantity, and heart in every batch.

Thank you for being part of our journey. Explore our menu, place an order, or just stop by for a free sample—we’d love to meet you!

Stay sweet,

Choose & Enjoy

Artisan French Baguette

Our golden-crusted baguettes are baked fresh daily using traditional French techniques. Enjoy the perfect balance of crispy exterior and airy interior, made with just four simple ingredients: flour, water, salt, and our secret natural leaven.

Classic Cinnamon Roll

Our signature cinnamon rolls feature soft, buttery dough swirled with cinnamon-sugar, baked to golden perfection, and topped with creamy vanilla glaze. Made fresh throughout the day for that warm, gooey experience.

Classic Chocolate Chip Cookie

our signature cookies feature premium chocolate chunks in a buttery, golden-brown dough with crisp edges and a soft center. Baked fresh daily using real vanilla and a pinch of sea salt for the perfect sweet-salty balance.

Artisan Croissants

Choose from our daily selection: classic butter, almond-filled, chocolate, or ham & cheese. Each made with our 48-hour fermented dough for exceptional flavor and texture.

Chocolate Cupcake

Daily rotating flavors including red velvet with cream cheese frosting, chocolate espresso, salted caramel, and seasonal specials. All made with premium ingredients and finished with decorative flair.

Artisan Donut Collection

Choose from our daily selection: classic glaze, chocolate frosted, cinnamon sugar, or creative seasonal specials. All made with premium ingredients and finished with hand-applied toppings.

Artisan Éclairs

Indulge in our daily selection: classic chocolate, coffee cream, seasonal fruit-filled, or salted caramel. Made with authentic French techniques using premium Valrhona chocolate and Madagascar vanilla.

Morning Muffin Collection

Choose from our daily baked varieties: classic blueberry, double chocolate, banana nut, or lemon poppyseed. All made with real ingredients - no mixes, just wholesome goodness in every muffin.

Farm Fresh Fruit Pies

Enjoy our rotating selection of pies made with seasonal fruits: summer berry medley, autumn pumpkin spice, or winter spiced pear. All featuring our signature lard-and-butter crust for perfect flakiness.

Artisan Sourdough Collection

Choose from our daily baked varieties: classic white, whole wheat, rosemary & olive oil, or seeded multigrain. All made with organic flour and naturally leavened for maximum flavor and digestibility.
